Annaelle started her undergraduate studies in Biology at the [University of Lille](https://www.univ-lille.fr/en/) (2022-2024), France. Last year, she joined the Magistère Européen de Génétique at the [University of Paris Cité](https://u-paris.fr/language/en/) (2024-present), specializing in genetics and molecular biology. During this time, she completed her bachelor’s degree with an internship at the [Jabado lab](https://www.jabadolab.com) in Montréal, Canada, where she studied the role and recruitment of the Menin-KMT2A complex in pediatric brain tumors. Now pursuing her Master’s studies, she has (temporarily) left behind the baguette and joined the Pauli lab in Vienna, where she is very excited to work on mRNA stability in killifish under the supervision of Ida. Outside the lab, Annaelle explores the real world on her bike and fictional ones on her console.
