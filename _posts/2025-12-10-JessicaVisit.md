During her PhD in the Pauli lab Jessica discovered that cells are guided by a [self-generated gradient](https://www.science.org/doi/10.1126/sciadv.add2488) during gastrulation in zebrafish. Cephalopods, like the octopus, stand out for their incredible intelligence, yet how their nervous system is formed remains largely unexplored. Jessica gave an exciting talk about the many interesting questions her PostDoc has opened up and started to deliver answers for.
