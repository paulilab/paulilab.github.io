Big congratulations to **Laura**, former postdoc and now independent group leader at the IMB in Mainz, for receiving the Biochemical Society Early Career Research Award 2027!

Laura received this award in recognition of her research performed as a postdoc in the Pauli lab, where she successfully bridged the gap between biochemistry and developmental biology to discover how ribosomes and mRNAs are stored in the zebrafish egg.

We are really happy for this well-deserved award for Laura, and are cheering for her and her new lab from Vienna!
