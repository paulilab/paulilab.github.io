Congratulations to **Ezra** who has been awarded a Marie Skłodowska-Curie Postdoctoral Fellowship. During his postdoc he will investigate how the germplasm changes during early development and how the genetic information stored inside it is activated to guide germline formation. A big round of applause to Ezra for securing this prestigious fellowship!

You can read more about Ezra’s project in the IMP [news post](https://www.imp.ac.at/news/article/ezra-levy-awarded-marie-sklodowska-curie-postdoctoral-fellowship).
