This week the Pauli lab moved - but just one floor up within the IMP. 
This gives us a chance to be closer to our fish facility and synergize on equipment and expertise with our new neighbors in the quadrant, the [Pinheiro lab](https://www.imp.ac.at/groups/diana-pinheiro) - our fellow zebrafish lab, the [Plaschka lab](https://www.imp.ac.at/groups/clemens-plaschka) and the [Balzarotti lab](https://www.imp.ac.at/groups/francisco-balzarotti). 
This move was expertly organized by **Karin** and **Carina**, and after a day of carrying cupboards and freezer contents up the stairs we all had a well-deserved cake break on the VBC terrace.
