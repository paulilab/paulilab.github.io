**Theresa** and **Gabi** attended the [Ubiquitin and Friends symposium](https://www.protein-degradation.org/symposium/) in Vienna this week, where Theresa presented her work on proteasome activity during early embryogenesis at the poster and flash talk session.
The conference featured exciting talks covering topics from ubiquitin to protein folding and ended with a conference dinner at a Heurigen.
