We were delighted to welcome the Ambassador of El Salvador to Austria, Mr. Kennedy Reyes, to the IMP and the Pauli lab. During his visit, Victoria presented her research on fertilization, and together with Andi, guided a tour of our fish facility and the Vienna BioCenter campus.
We greatly appreciate this opportunity to connect and exchange ideas, and we look forward to continuing to attract talented students from El Salvador and across Latin America to our campus through initiatives such as the Talents-for-Future program and the VBC PhD Program.
