From IMP to IMB - the Pauli lab has its first real 'offspring'!
From 1st February 2026, the [**Lorenzo-Orts** lab](https://www.imb.de/research/our-research-groups/lorenzo-orts-lab/research) opened its doors at the [IMB](https://www.imb.de/) in Mainz in Germany, led by our (now former) postdoc **Laura**.
The Lorenzo-Orts lab will investigate how maternal mRNAs are regulated and activated during embryogensis, using both in vivo and in vitro approaches. We are super excited to find out what Laura will discover with her lab in the future, and wish her the very best for a great start into independence!

Here is a link to an [interview](https://www.imp.ac.at/news/article/from-imp-postdocs-to-group-leaders-laura-lorenzo-orts-and-ulrich-hohmann-start-their-labs-at-imb-mainz) with Laura and Ulrich about their move from being postdocs at the Vienna BioCenter to leading their own labs at the IMB. 

Big congratulations to Laura and Ulrich for having achieved this double-feature!
