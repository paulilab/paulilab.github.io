Congrats to **Anastasia**, whose collaborative work on identifying the function of the microprotein SMIM26 with the lab of Gunter Meister at the University of Regensburg is now published in Genes & Development!
In the new manuscript we found that SMIM26 connects metabolite transporters of the outer and inner mitochondrial membranes, which is essential for respiratory chain function. 

Check out the story here: [Paper link](https://doi.org/10.1101/gad.353272.125)
