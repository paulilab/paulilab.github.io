We are happy to announce that our discovery of the **SPARK complex** is now out on bioRxiv! This has been a big collaborative effort led by the [Pauli lab](https://www.imp.ac.at/groups/andrea-pauli) (IMP, Vienna) and [Ikawa lab](https://www.biken.osaka-u.ac.jp/en/laboratories/detail/17) (University of Osaka, Japan), uncovering a conserved fertilizatioan complex in mouse and zebrafish that couples sperm-egg binding to membrane fusion. 
Congrats to the two joint first authors **Victoria** and **Johannes** for leading this work and everyone else involved! 

Follow the link to the [bioRxiv pre-print](https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.64898/2026.05.13.724128v1) to find out more.
